Above are comparison images for NGC3031-field23. Hold your cursor over one of the three colored rectangles of each section (or the mask button) to see the image. Clicking on a colored rectangle will open the source image as a large (~10 MB) jpeg file. The completeness (Comp.) of the artificial star tests sections indicates the percentage of generated stars recovered given the selection culls of the pipeline. The F606W offset indicates the difference between the F606W input magnitude of the artificial star and the magnitude subsequently measured by the pipeline. The photometric catalogs and further analysis can be found further down the page.
