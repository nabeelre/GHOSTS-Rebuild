Welcome to the GHOSTS Website

This is the website for the Galaxy Halos, Outer disks, Substructure, Thick disks and Star clusters (GHOSTS) survey. The PI of the project is Roelof de Jong.

An overview of the survey can be read here, whilst a list of team members can be found here. Publications from the project are listed here and the current status of the survey is continually update here.

This site may be navigated using the links at the top right of every page.
