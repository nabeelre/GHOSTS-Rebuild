<p2>Below are comparison images for NGC7814-field03. Hold your cursor
over one of the three segments of each dial (or the mask button) to
see the image. Clicking on the segment will open the source image as
a large (~10 MB) jpeg file. The completeness (Comp.) segment of the
artificial star tests dial indicates the percentage of generated
stars recovered given the selection culls of the pipeline. The F606W
offset indicates the difference between the F606W input magnitude of
the artificial star and the magnitude subsequently measured by the
pipeline. The photometric catalogs and further analysis can be found
further down the page.<br>&nbsp</p2>
